Recipe #1

Homeade Scrambled Eggs

  • Time: 8 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 6
Butter Enough to grease the pan
Salt & Pepper Add at will

Step 1: Crack the eggs into a bowl and mix them until the solution
is homogenous.

Step 2: Heat up pan with butter in it. Move the melting butter
around to coat all or most of the bottom of the pan.

Step 3: Pour the egg mix into the pan and stir constantly.

Step 4: Add salt and pepper if you want to.

Step 3: Turn heat off and serve the eggs when they are at the
consistency you enjoy!

Recipe #2

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves 1

Grilled Cheese

Ingredients Quantity
Sliced Bread 2 slices
Sliced Cheese Enough to cover the bread
Butter Enough to cover pan and bread

Step 1: Put the butter on the pan and let it melt.

Step 2: While the butter is melting, put the cheese on the bread.

Step 3: Wait until the bread is slightly burnt looking on one side.

Step 4:Flip the sandwich and wait until it is the right color for you.

Step 5: Eat up!

Recipe #3

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2

Avocado Quesadilla

Ingredients Quantity
Corn tortilla 6
Cheese Enough to cover the Tortillas
Avocado 3-4 depending on the sizes

Step 1: Grate/slice the cheese onto the tortillas

Step 2: Heat the pan and put the tortillas onto it

Step 3: While the tortilla is heating up, slice the avocados

Step 4: When the cheese is melted on the tortilla, put the tortilla on
your plate and put the avocado on it.
